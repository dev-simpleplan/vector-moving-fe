MOVING PERKS

What’s Included In Vector’s Transparent Moving Pricing

Honesty matters at Vector. We provide a clear fee structure for our residential moving services. Once your estimate is locked in, that's what you’ll pay – no hidden fees ever.

Plus, we always include:

  • Packing Essentials like boxes, tape, and bubble wrap to ensure your valuables are safe and secure throughout the journey.
  • Furniture Protection by meticulously wrapping your furniture with high-quality padding for an extra layer of protection from scratches and dings
  • Wall and Floor Protection to protect your old and new home from damage during the moving process.
  • One Flat Fee that covers everything – travel time for our experienced movers, the labor involved in loading, unloading, and transporting your belongings.
